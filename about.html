History

Rafting Adventure was born from the passion of a young outdoor enthusiast, Jake Thompson. Growing up near the rushing rivers of Veracruz, Jake spent his childhood exploring their currents, dreaming of sharing the thrill of rafting with others. After a life-changing expedition through the Veracruz River, he decided to turn his dream into reality. In 2005, he started with just two rafts and a small team of guides. Word quickly spread about their exhilarating trips and top-notch safety standards. Today, Rafting Adventure is a renowned company, offering unforgettable river journeys to adventurers from all over the world.
